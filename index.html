Conundrum of web metadata:

  • Loosely-declared metadata can lead to ambiguity
  • Structured and descriptive metadata is burdensome

What is the Semantic Web?

  • Highly formalized metadata
  • Layered on top of the open World Wide Web
  • Data that can refer to other data (linkage)
  • Data that software can denote (descriptive meaning)
  • Data that can be reasoned about (structural meaning)

Why?

  • Enable decentralized, autonomous software agents
  • Enable data to be easily and cheaply aggregated
  • Create a "value chain" of data

Descriptive Meaning

Denote assertions in data with types and properties.
  • Case of ambiguity:
    "It depends upon what the meaning of the word 'is' is."
      - A prominent attorney
  • Assertion:
    "Los Angeles is a city"
  • Assertion on the Semantic Web:
    'Los Angeles' | is a type of | https://schema.org/City

Descriptive Meaning - Standards

  • W3C RDF Schema: "Type"
  • W3C OWL: "Object Property"
  • Example from Pizza Ontology
    https://protege.stanford.edu/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl
  • "a topping is a functional property of a pizza"

Structural Meaning

To be able to reason about data, specify the relations among types.

Structural Meaning - Standards

  • W3C RDF Schema: "Type"
  • W3C OWL: "Transitive Property"
  • Example from Pizza Ontology
    https://protege.stanford.edu/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl
  • "the ingredients of ingredients are ingredients of the whole" 

...
<!--
 http://www.co-ode.org/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl#hasIngredient 
-->
<owl:ObjectProperty rdf:about="http://www.co-ode.org/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl#hasIngredient">
<owl:inverseOf rdf:resource="http://www.co-ode.org/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl#isIngredientOf"/>
<rdf:type rdf:resource="http://www.w3.org/2002/07/owl#TransitiveProperty"/>
<rdfs:domain rdf:resource="http://www.co-ode.org/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl#Food"/>
<rdfs:range rdf:resource="http://www.co-ode.org/ontologies/pizza/pizza.owl#Food"/>
<rdfs:comment xml:lang="en">
NB Transitive - the ingredients of ingredients are ingredients of the whole
</rdfs:comment>
</owl:ObjectProperty>

Transitive Property

  • Mini hot dog is an ingredient of pizza.
  • Ketchup is an ingredient of mini hot dog.
  • Therefore, ketchup is an ingredient of pizza.
